Mental Health Test

Assess your Mental Health with these questions and get free advice.


1. Do you often feel overwhelmed or stressed?

2. Do you struggle with sleep (too little or too much)?

3. Have you lost interest in activities you once enjoyed?

4. Do you often feel tired or lacking in energy?

5. Have you experienced frequent mood swings?

6. Do you often feel lonely or isolated?

7. Have you experienced difficulty concentrating or making decisions?

8. Do you often feel anxious or worried?

9. Have you had thoughts of self-harm or harming others?

10. Do you feel hopeless about the future?

Please answer all questions before submitting.
